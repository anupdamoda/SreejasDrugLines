Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Website building Designing, Development, Process and Pricing

How much do you charge for the website?
Our websites range from $599 for basic websites up to $2,999 or more for larger and more complex sites. We aim to keep our prices competitive and fair, so a great website is within reach of all businesses of any size. We also offer flexible monthly support packages to ensure your website is always in top shape! These packages range from $99 to $399 per month and include website hosting, management, backups, reporting, technical support and on-going website changes. See our pricing page for more information.
How long does it take to build the website?
Not as long as you might think! Our website build times can take as little as 5-10 days for mini and small websites, with most medium and large custom websites taking 15-25 days.
Why should I choose Ace Global Automation to build my website?
With so many digital agencies in the market and so much information out there on website do’s and don’ts, we aim to KEEP DIGITAL SIMPLE. If you want to deal with a no-nonsense agency who will work with you to understand your business and deliver personalised service with a fast turnaround, then Ace Global Automation may be the right fit for you.
What kind of businesses do you work with?
We work with a wide range of businesses both large and small. We specialise in the service sector, particularly in trades, hospitality and professional services. However, we are always happy to take on new clients in other industries. Please contact us to find out how we can help your business.
How does the building website process work?
Our process is very straightforward and transparent. Once you contact us, we will call you for a chat so we can understand you and what your needs are. Once we have all the information we need, we will send you a detailed proposal for your new website. If you accept our proposal and pay the initial deposit we then get straight to work wireframing your new website, during which time you will provide us with the content you would like included. We then workshop our initial design with you so you can provide feedback and any required changes for us to make. This feedback and change process may occur more than once depending on the size and complexity of your website. Once the design is finalised, we then fully test and launch the website and provide you with a handover pack. At this time you pay the remaining balance and we then activate your pre-selected monthly website support package.
Are your websites mobile friendly?
Absolutely! Our websites are designed to look fantastic on any screen, whether it is a mobile phone, tablet, laptop or desktop computer.
